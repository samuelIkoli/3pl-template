Phoenix Park Projects Limited

Phoenix Park Projects Limited (3PL) is a technology-driven company incorporated in Nigeria to deliver digital and physical platforms using available local resources, knowledge and skills to promote industrialization. The company's priority sectors are housing, agriculture, manufacturing, mining, renewable energy and infrastructure. 3PL has a philosophy to blend economic development, technological innovation, and environmental harmony. The Company seeks to build industrial and commercial facilities, ICT and knowledge hubs, recreation, and homes to facilitate social interactions and prosperity. 3PL brings global knowledge, years of cognate experience, and multi-disciplinary capabilities to serve private and public clients. The company and its network of seasoned partners and associates with diverse experiences are driven by a passion to deliver transformative Projects of high impact and outcomes, and change the world for a better place. 3PL offer services to Clients to successfully execute complex industrial and infrastructure projects and skills training for optimal performance.

Consultancy Services

3PL possesses the capacity and experience to provide all of studies, the technical design, and estimation services required to implement projects in its business areas. Every project is unique and has requirements specific to the client, business, and country. 3PL members and associates are multidisciplinary professionals based in various regions of the world with domain knowledge and broad experience, and a detailed understanding of the services. 3PL also provides adviceand support for developing and realizing new project ideas. Working with investors, clients, financial entities, and project developers the company conducts rigorous assessments and analyses of a given project's technical, operational, financial, and economic constraints before making investment decisions. The complexity and sophistication of Clients' demands have become increasingly dynamic in today's world. Customer and employee expectations are changing, requiring current skill sets to manage these changes. 3PL Advisory professionals develop customized strategies for Clients and help companies stay ahead of the game with bold and strategic thinking, big ideas and progressive approaches. Our outside-in, customer-led approach facilitates the agility and innovation necessary to be a future-ready organization with a focus on pragmatism. Professional project management is necessary to reach project goals regarding time, quality, and cost. The 3PL team has many years of collective experience in preparing and executing large-scale, interdisciplinary projects. 3PL has domain knowledge, capacity, and expertise to perform project management tasks as a prime consultant. 3PL carries out studies in climate mitigation, adaptation, biodiversity recovery, and transition to net-zero carbon emissions. These are at the top-line of every national ant transnational agenda today. Pollution, over exploitation of natural resources, environmental degradation have led to severe pervasive, and possibly irreversible changes for people, assets, economies, and ecosystems worldwide. These adverse trends must be stopped and where possible reversed.
