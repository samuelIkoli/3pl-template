About Us

Global impact for your business

The experience of our professional team shall be deployed to ensure customer's satisfaction is guaranteed.

MISSION

The creation of digital knowledge and physical platforms to enable innovation, and prosperity in the ecosystems.

VISION

To facilitate sustainable economic transformation by delivering on the full potentials of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) while building a culture of excellence in environmental compliance, corporate practice, and ethics.

WHO ARE WE?

Phoenix Park Projects Limited (3PL), a company incorporated in Nigeria, is a technology-driven company based on digital and physical platforms focused on knowledge, industrialization and agriculture, as a complete ecosystem. The Company’s network of seasoned partners and associates with diverse experience is driven by a passion to deliver transformative projects of high impact and outcomes. 3PL has a philosophy to blend economic development, technological innovation, and environmental harmony. The Company seeks to build industrial and commercial facilities, ICT and knowledge hubs, recreation, and homes to facilitate social interactions and prosperity. 3PL aspires to be the think tank for research, development, implementation, and be the managing entity for a contingent of core industrialists and businessmen in individual African nations. The African business community can effectively participate and take ownership of the African Continent Free Trade Agreement (AfCFTA).
