About Us

Global impact for your business

The experience of our professional team shall be deployed to ensure customer's satisfaction is guaranteed.

VISION

To facilitate sustainable economic transformation by delivering on the full potentials of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) while building a culture of excellence in environmental compliance, corporate practice, and ethics.

MISSION

The creation of digital knowledge and physical platforms to enable innovation, and prosperity in the ecosystems.

WHO ARE WE?

Phoenix Park Projects Limited (3PL), a company incorporated in Nigeria, is a technology-driven company based on digital and physical platforms focused on knowledge, industrialization and agriculture, as a complete ecosystem. The Company’s network of seasoned partners and associates with diverse experience is driven by a passion to deliver transformative projects of high impact and outcomes. 3PL has a philosophy to blend economic development, technological innovation, and environmental harmony. The Company seeks to build industrial and commercial facilities, ICT and knowledge hubs, recreation, and homes to facilitate social interactions and prosperity. 3PL aspires to be the think tank for research, development, implementation, and be the managing entity for a contingent of core industrialists and businessmen in individual African nations. The African business community can effectively participate and take ownership of the African Continent Free Trade Agreement (AfCFTA).

PRIORITY SECTOR

3PL has carefully and intentionally selected priority sectors based on expected high impact and outcome on national development. The sectors are drivers of growth with accelerated income generation capacity and potential for promotion of circular economy. Below are the sectors we focus on:

Agriculture
Given the country’s comparative advantage and its multiplier effect on the economy investment in this sector ought to be promoted to reverse the high import bills in the nation by utilizing favorable and available resources as well as using improved inputs and innovative agricultural technologies.
Housing
3PL aims to reduce the housing deficit through technology, reduce cost by innovation, improve quality and energy efficiency within the housing sector are the drivers of ensuring growth.
Mineral Resources and Mining
Take advantage of the abundance of raw materials by sourcing capital and technology to profitably exploiting these valuable resources. 3PL shall promote resource mapping and collaborative mining exploitation with relevant international stakeholders.
Infrastructure Development
3Pl aims to propose an intermediate in the identification and development if critical Infrastructure necessary for national and regional economic and industrial growth.
Oil and Gas
Oil and gas provides the energy necessary for powering the industrial and economic growth of the nation. 3PL will seek to utilize these resources available in our land optimally by promoting modular scalable projects at appropriate locations.
Chemical and Petrochemical
3PL intend to master the mother manufacturing and production at the most economic cost.
Digital technology / ICT/Digital Entrepreneurship
The digital economy has become invaluable to economic growth. Through our young and innovative workforce ensure that the momentum in the digital space is further enhanced and utilized to promote production, finance, communication and general socio-economic development.
Climate & Environment
3PL carries out studies and promotes projects that limit global warming, adapt to the unavoidable impacts of climate change, combat environmental degradation, halt biodiversity loss, and, and stop widening inequalities.
Financial Services
Ensure that we broaden the financial space by ensuring that financial services are faster, customer focused and inclusive.
Tourism
Showcase the diversity of land, peoples and culture within Nigeria visible to the world and make economic gains from doing so.
